About

BINI is a dynamic eight-member Filipino girl group formed under ABS-CBN's Star Hunt Academy. Comprising the talented members Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena, they have quickly risen to stardom with their infectious energy and captivating performances.

BINI's impact extends far beyond their musical talent. Their infectious optimism and empowering messages have resonated deeply with audiences, making them a beacon of hope in today's challenging times. The group's biggest hit, "Pantropiko," became an anthem of unity and resilience, capturing the hearts of millions and serving as a testament to their ability to create music that truly connects with people. Through their music, BINI has fostered a sense of community and inspired countless individuals to pursue their passions and dreams.
